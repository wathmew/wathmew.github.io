ShareLM

ShareLM is a Chrome plugin that lets you contribute your chatbot conversations to a dataset that exists for the benefit of the open-source community. Here's the paper for it!

I worked on it as part of EleutherAI's Summer of Open AI Research and I added support for ChatGPT's code canvases.

Since all the private companies are taking your chat logs as training data anyway (unless you opt out), you might as well contribute it! Plus, I like that ShareLM gives you more agency over your data; before it's sent to the database, it exists in your local Chrome storage for 24 hours and you can choose to remove it from the batch that will be uploaded.
